Privacy Statement

Heartry by Darshan Rander

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us.

We do not collect any personal data.

Your data will be stored on your device and on your Google Drive (if you opt-in for a Drive backup).

In case the app crashes, you will be given a choice to share the crash details with us, which will include some of your device's specifications such as screen size, Android version, device model, SoC company and crash report. These specifications will NOT be sufficient to uniquely identify you or your device.

We do not use trackers.
