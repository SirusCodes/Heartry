Heartry

By Darshan Rander

Heartry is an app for poets and writers to seamlessly write and share their work in plain text or as images!
The app boasts features such as:
  • Shareable images: Directly share poems/stories on various social media platforms as images. Easily change the font size, font colour and background gradient of the images.
  • Reader mode: Easily read stored poems/stories without the interference of the cursor and keyboard.
  • Theme: Light, dark and black
  • A clean and simple UI
  • A focus on privacy (Our policy)
  • No advertisements
Upcoming features:
  • Custom background image for the shareable images
  • List of ready-made colour schemes for the shareable images
  • Save custom background gradients for the shareable images
  • Shareable links to stories/poems in the app
  • Backup to Google Drive
  • Search
